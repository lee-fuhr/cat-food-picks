How we choose our picks

We don’t run lab tests ourselves. Instead, we gather information from long-term owner forums, published veterinary reviews, and expert analyses that track how a food performs over months and years. We look for consistent positive feedback on coat condition, digestion, and palatability, and we note where owners report issues like ingredient changes or packaging problems. Our picks are the result of cross-referencing dozens of sources and weighing trade-offs, no food is perfect for every cat. We prioritize brands with transparent sourcing and a track record of meeting AAFCO standards, and we always consider real-world affordability and availability so your choice is both sound and sustainable.
